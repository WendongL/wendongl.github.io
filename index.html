Wendong Liang

publishes under Liang Wendong.

I am a PhD student in the Empirical Inference department at Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, under the supervision of Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf. I am interested in the alignment and interpretability in machine learning.

I hold an MSc in Mathematics from ENS Paris-Saclay, where I also earned my BSc. During my studies, I interned at the Innsbruck University, working on categorical probability and completed my master’s thesis at Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. In addition, I hold a BA in French Language and Western Philosophy from Nankai University.
